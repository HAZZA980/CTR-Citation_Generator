Checklists: a way to build effective study

Try using some the suggestions below when gathering information and materials for an assignment. 

  • I take the initiative action to find out everything I need to know about my study 
    • Use the Start of the academic year collection and the following topic from Getting ready for academic study How can I prepare
  • I plan assignments well-ahead
    • You might find Working backwards from deadlines helpful when planning your assignments
  • I start to prepare early for tests and exams 
    • Apply some of the techniques from the topic Exam success when preparing for tests and exams
  • I prepare my day and week in advance 
    • Use this topic to Set your priorities
  • I use my time effectively  
    • Find out ways you can manage your time, using the topic 7 steps to effective time management
  • I use a diary/planner effectively  
    • Evaluate the ways that you can schedule your time using the topic Make your planning work for you
  • I use technology effectively 
    • Here are some ways to get started: Studying online collection 
  • I understand the conventions of higher education and academic study, and how these relate to the course I am studying
    • Explore the Start of the academic year collection to support you
  • To avoid cheating and plagiarism  
    • Have a look at the topic How to avoid plagiarism and Ways of using other writers' text from the Referencing and plagiarism module
