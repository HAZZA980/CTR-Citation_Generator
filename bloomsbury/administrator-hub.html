Welcome to the Skills for Study administrator hub

Skills for Study will help your students develop the essential skills they require in order to succeed on their course and beyond!

Within this administrator hub, you'll find options to customise your Skills for Study subscription, pull usage reports, integrate Skills for Study with your VLE/LMS, generate deep-links to content and manage user accounts.

If you have any questions or require support, please contact your institution's Skills for Study Administrator, or email us at:

  • UK, Europe, Middle East, Africa, Asia: OnlineSalesUK@bloomsbury.com
  • Americas: OnlineSalesUS@bloomsbury.com
  • Australia and New Zealand: OnlineSalesANZ@bloomsbury.com

Note about viewing usage statistics for the first time

Please note if you are accessing site usage page for the very first time:

  • Expect the statistics generation process to take longer than usual (30-60 seconds) for this one-off occasion
  • On subsequent revisits, the usage page will load as normal and much quicker
