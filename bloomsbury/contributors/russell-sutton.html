Russell Sutton

Russell Sutton studied Journalism and Advertising at Ohio University, USA; his thesis investigated the effect of the Great Depression on retail advertising. He worked in advertising for over thirty years, and designed and taught courses in Communication at Philadelphia University for a decade. Prior to retiring, Russell taught Language Arts at a middle school in Florida. He is now a freelance writer.
